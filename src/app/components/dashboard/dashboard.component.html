Enrollment Status Description

Enrollment Status

Description

For EnlistmentYou are not done with the enlistment process.
Enlisment, For schedulingYou are already done with the Enlisment Process. Waiting for the schedule to be given by the coordinator.
Scheduled You have been given schedule by the coordinator of your respective program. Waiting for the approval of the registrar.
Officially EnrolledYour enrollment has been approved by the registrar.
Will Not EnrollYou marked yourself as will not enroll for the semester OR you've been automatically mark as WILL NOT ENROLL because you have not done the enlistment process.
NOT Re-AdmittedSomehow you've been denied admission in your chosen program. Most common reason is that you have failed to pass the retention policy of the program you are in. You may choose to try another program.
